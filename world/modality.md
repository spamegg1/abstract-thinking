# Modalities

## Aspects of modalities

We can vary the following aspects.

### The nature of knowledge

- "something is true"
- "*I know* something is true"
- "something is *provable*"

### The nature of truth

- *necessary truth*
- *possible truth*
- *contingent truth*

### The type or goal of the activity

- *calculating*
- *proving*
- *exploring*
- *guessing / heuristics*

### The accuracy

- *deterministic*
- *probabilistic*
- *stochastic*
- *exact*
- *approximate*
